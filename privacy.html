Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Introduction

At Mnemo, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application and website. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

We personally do not collect any information that leaves your device. Everything is stored locally on your device. We do however use certain thirdparty services that may collect information. These services include:

  • AI Providers: We use AI providers to generate content for the application. This includes the AI used in the learning paths, flashcards, quizzes, and more. Somethings are done locally, but when AI features are activated it may send data to the AI provider.
  • Extensions and Games We have an open system where developers can create extensions and games. These extensions and games may collect information from you accessable through the mnemo api. We do not have control over what they do with the data, so we can not be held responsible for their actions. However, everything released to the mnemo store is reviewed by us before it is published, but users are able to import games and extensions from the internet, which we have to insight to.

How We Use Your Information

The data we collect thats stored locally on your devices includes:

  • User created content: Flashcards, mindmaps, learning paths, games, extensions, etc.
  • Settings: Theme, language, etc.
  • Analytics: Retention rate, usage, etc used to provide statistics to the user and for core functions to work, such as the flashcard algorithm which uses things such as retention rate to determine what cards to show next.

Data Security

Data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your data, and we do not store any data on our servers. We do however use thirdparty services that may collect information. We also might send https requests such as video parsing, inseration of images, etc.

Third-Party Services

We retain the right to collect user data for analytics and core functions. We do not sell your data to thirdparties, and we do not share your data with thirdparties unless you have given us permission to do so. We do however use thirdparty services that may collect information. We also might send https requests such as video parsing, inseration of images, etc. The data is anonymized and not linked to you as a user.

Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.
