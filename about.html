About mnemo

Our Mission

Access powerful learning tools, now open source and free.

Mnemo was born from a simple idea: learning should be accessible, engaging, and effective for everyone. We're changing how people learn by providing powerful, open-source tools that adapt to learners, not for profit.

Accessibility First

We believe that quality education tools should be available to everyone, regardless of their circumstances.

Innovation Driven

We continuously explore new ways to make learning more effective and engaging through technology.

Community Powered

Our strength comes from our diverse community of learners, educators, and developers.

Why mnemo?

The motivation behind the project

My journey to becoming a doctor, fueled by a deep passion for science, has taught me the invaluable power of education. I've seen how essential learning is and the impact when access is denied or lacking. Yet, many companies prioritize profit, locking vital study tools behind steep paywalls and hindering student success.

I am building Mnemo to challenge this. We need a better path forward:

  • No more expensive subscriptions for fundamental learning tools.
  • Customizable software that truly fits your study needs.
  • Your data remains private and under your control.
  • Features driven by students, not corporates pockets
  • Open source software for everyone, regardless of their financial situation.

mnemo is more than just another study app - it's a statement that quality education tools should be accessible to everyone. By making mnemo open source, we ensure that it will always remain free and that its development will always be guided by the needs of learners, not shareholders.
