Frequently Asked Questions

Is mnemo really free?

Yes! mnemo is and will always be completely free and open-source. Donations are entirely optional and help support development.

Where does my donation go?

Your donations directly support mnemo's development by covering costs of development tools, hosting, and infrastructure, we are not doing this for profit.

Will I get anything in return?

While donations don't provide extra features (mnemo is fully featured for everyone), you'll get our eternal gratitude and the knowledge that you're helping make education free and accessible for everyone.
